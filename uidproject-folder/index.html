How to Use

1

I Need Help

Click the red "I Need Help" button. Fill in your name, what happened, your location, and phone number. Then press Submit.

2

I Can Help

Click the green "I Can Help" button. Enter your name, what you can offer (food, medicine, shelter), and how much. Then press Submit.

3

See All Requests

Click "Feed" in the menu to see all emergency requests and available resources near you.

4

View on Map

Click "Map" in the menu to see all requests and volunteers shown on a live map near your location.
